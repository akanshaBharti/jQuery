B.Sc. Hons Computer Science

Course Details

Fee: Contact the university for detailed fee information.

Eligibility Criteria: Candidates should have completed 10+2 with a strong background in Mathematics and Computer Science.

Core Papers

  • Introduction to Computer Science (Credits: 4)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms (Credits: 5)
  • Database Management Systems (Credits: 4)
  • Operating Systems (Credits: 4)
  • Computer Networks (Credits: 4)

Elective Papers

  • Artificial Intelligence (Credits: 4)
  • Web Development (Credits: 4)
  • Mobile App Development (Credits: 4)

Future Possibilities

Future Possibilities: Graduates can pursue careers in software development, data science, cybersecurity, and more. They can also opt for higher studies such as M.Sc. or M.Tech.
